About us

We are a dynamic and customer oriented Organization established to provide a single source for all types of Piping materials, Chemicals and Industrial spares.

Established in 2009 by professionals working in supply chain industry from two decades, OGS and Chemicals has emerged as a leading trade house in a short span of time. We cater to wider markets of Middle east and Africa, and are registered vendors to major international & local Oil, Gas and Petrochemical plants.

We import and stock Quality Chemicals, Oilfield and Industrial parts from U.S.A, Japan, European Union, India, Korea & China and represent some of the manufacturers exclusively for their complete product range.
